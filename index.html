Biography

I'm currently a freelance student, researcher, and coder with interests in pure mathematics and computer science. My main passion has always been learning, so I find myself engaging with many different disciplines(physics, engineering, philosophy) based on my current interests. I'm currently preparing to enroll in a Ph.D. mathematics program and seeking an industrial job solving problems to pay the bills. At the same time, I greatly enjoy building things, as evidenced in this site and my personal hobbies such as vehicle repair and computer hardware. From proofs of the most abstract mathematical concepts to rebuilding an ignition system, I am constantly pursuing new challenges and problems to ponder and conquer. I'm more than happy to do this on my own, but financial considerations motivate me to seek out like-minded individuals with similar passion and dedication. Please take a look at some of the work I've done and experiences I've had to find out if your current problems seem like ones we could benefit from solving together. Thanks!
